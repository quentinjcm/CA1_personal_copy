        reflected colour = trace new ray in reflection direction

refracted light:
    if isTransparnt & ray deoth < 3
        calulate refraction direction
        refracted colour = trace new ray in refraction direction
    else
        refracted colour = blinn shading for the point

clamp refracred and reflectd light

lightOut = reflection amount * reflected colour + (1 - reflection amount) * refracted colour
